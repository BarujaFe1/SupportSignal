# SupportSignal — Portfolio Pitch

## Elevator (PT-BR)
SupportSignal é uma camada de inteligência para operações de suporte pequenas: classifica mensagens, mede SLA, aponta risco de reembolso e transforma sintomas em causa raiz e plano de ação — sem substituir o helpdesk.

## Elevator (EN)
SupportSignal is an intelligence layer for small support ops: it classifies messages, measures SLA, scores refund risk and turns symptoms into root causes and an action plan — without replacing the helpdesk.

## Demo narrative
1. Import demo inbox (CSV / synthetic tickets)
2. Show topic mix and emerging themes
3. Highlight SLA breaches
4. Open refund risk board
5. Walk root-cause explorer
6. Close with weekly memo + action backlog

## Interview talking points
- Differentiation: intelligence layer, not another ticket tool
- Pragmatic AI: configurable classification + human review for high risk
- Privacy: masked previews, local-first import for MVP
- Monetization: volume tiers + usage-based analysis
